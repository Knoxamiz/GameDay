## Description

GameDay helps youth sports organizations, coaches, and families stay aligned
before, during, and after game day.

Admins can create organization or single-team workspaces, build rosters, publish
registration links, assign coaches, schedule events, and send organization or
team updates. Coaches get focused access to assigned teams, active rosters,
schedule context, attendance, and parent communication. Parents see their
players first, with the next thing each player needs: registration status,
schedule, attendance, transportation, and missing requirements.

GameDay is built around verified account access. Organizations, teams, players,
registrations, schedules, documents, and messages stay scoped to the right
workspace so users do not have to hunt through unrelated tools.
